Research

The above picture is a 200:1 scaled planetary gearbox to be used on a jet engine. High frequency vibrations that occurs during the operation of the gearbox will significantly reduce the service life. A control system is therefore needed to sense the vibration and send back a counteractive force to cancel the vibration. I was able to design a Filtered least mean square algorithm and an AGA algorithm and implemented them in MATLAB simulink models and tested on the test rig. My genetic algorithm was able to reduce 50% of the vibration on average.
